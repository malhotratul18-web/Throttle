Throttle X Workshop

Vatva, Ahmedabad — since 2009

It started with one bike, one bench, and one stubborn idea — that every motorcycle deserves better than stock. Fifteen years later, we're still at it, except now we've got a full workshop, a crew of obsessives, and over two thousand builds to our name.

We don't take every job. We take the right ones — riders who care about how their machine sounds, sits, and feels under them. If that's you, we should talk.

How We Work

You come in, we look at the bike together, and figure out what it needs. No upselling, no fluff — just an honest conversation about what's possible with your budget and your vision.
